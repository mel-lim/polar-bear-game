Eat those fish!

Instructions

  1. To win, use the icebergs to help the polar bear cross the water to get the fish.
  2. To move the polar bear, use the arrow keys or the directional buttons at the bottom of the screen.
  3. If the polar bear falls into the water or gets carried off the screen by an iceberg, you lose.
  4. When you're ready, hit the space bar or click the "start/pause" button at the bottom of the screen to start playing.
